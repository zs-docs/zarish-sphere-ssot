# Maternal & Child Health

Comprehensive reproductive, maternal, newborn, and child health services.

## Modules

### 1. Antenatal Care (ANC)
- 8-contact ANC model
- Risk screening
- Ultrasound protocols
- Birth preparedness

### 2. Postnatal Care (PNC)
- Mother & baby assessments
- Breastfeeding support
- Postpartum complications
- Family planning integration

### 3. Child Health
- Growth monitoring
- Immunizations (EPI)
- IMCI protocols
- Developmental assessment

### 4. Nutrition
- Malnutrition screening
- Therapeutic feeding
- Micronutrient supplementation
- Community-based nutrition
