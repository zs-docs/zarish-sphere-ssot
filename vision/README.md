# Vision & Strategy

Strategic direction for the Zarish Sphere platform.

## Mission

To provide comprehensive, standardized healthcare documentation and management tools for low-resource settings worldwide.

## Vision

A world where every healthcare facility, regardless of resource constraints, has access to world-class clinical protocols, data management, and interoperability standards.

## Strategic Pillars

### 1. Clinical Excellence
- Evidence-based protocols
- WHO guideline alignment
- Continuous quality improvement

### 2. Technical Innovation
- FHIR standard adoption
- No-code platform development
- Automated deployment

### 3. Global Accessibility
- Open-source commitment
- Multi-language support
- Low-bandwidth optimization

### 4. Humanitarian Focus
- Refugee & conflict settings
- Emergency response
- Resource-constrained environments
