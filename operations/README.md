# Operational Modules

This directory contains all 7 operational modules for healthcare facility management.

## Modules

### 1. Human Resources
Staff management, scheduling, training, and performance management.

### 2. Finance & Budget
Financial management, accounting, procurement, and donor tracking.

### 3. Supply Chain
Inventory management, logistics, distribution, and stock control.

### 4. Infrastructure
Facility management, maintenance, utilities, and space planning.

### 5. Quality & Compliance
Quality assurance, audits, accreditation, and regulatory compliance.

### 6. Security
Access control, data protection, physical security, and incident response.

### 7. ICT (Information & Communication Technology)
IT infrastructure, system administration, help desk, and technical support.

## Integration

Operational modules integrate with:
- Clinical health modules
- FHIR resources
- Reporting systems
- Financial systems
